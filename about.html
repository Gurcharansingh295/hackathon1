About Our Project

The Medication Reminder Website helps users keep track of their medicines, dosage, and timings. It aims to support good health and well-being by ensuring you never miss an important dose.

This project is designed by **Gurcharan and Kaushik** as a simple health-support application focusing on user convenience and wellness.
